Risks - Detailed

Infections

Spyware, Malware, Trojans, Viruses, Keyloggers, Rootkits

An infection could be watching your keystrokes, clipboard or your screen.
Take your computer offline
An infection could be watching your keystrokes, clipboard or your screen, storing them on a hard drive while offline, to be broadcast later.

Take your computer offline permanently OR switch to a Live CD / USB

Your Network

Infected network nodes, Network intruders

An infected network device or a network intruder could intercept print jobs as they are sent.
Connect your printer to your computer directly

Your Printer

Printer history

If your printer keeps a history of print jobs, they could be taken by anyone with network access to it.
Connect your printer to your computer directly OR Ensure your printer does not save its history
If your printer keeps a history of print jobs, once the printer is brought online, the print jobs could be taken by anyone with network access to it.
Connect your printer to your computer directly, and never connect it to a network OR Ensure your printer does not save its history

Your Browser

Malicious plugins, Addons, Extensions

Malicious browser addons could be tracking everything that gets rendered on your screen.
Take your computer offline
Malicious browser addons could be tracking everything that gets rendered on your screen. If you're offline, these addons could store a history and transmit them when you connect again.
Take your computer offline permanently

Your OS

OS & 3rd party software vulnerabilities, Logs, Reporting

An attacker could be exploiting a vulnerability in your OS, giving them full access to your screen.
Take your computer offline
An attacker could be exploiting a vulnerability in your OS, recording your actions while offline, retrieving them when you connect again.
Take your computer offline permanently

This Tool

If this tool were malicious, or if you downloaded it from an unsafe source

(Only download this tool through CryptoPapers.com)
RNG pools or private keys could be broadcasted to an external server.
Take your computer offline
RNG pools or private keys could be stored as cookies, to be retrieved later by a website.
Turn off cookies OR Take your computer offline permanently
The RNG pool generation process could be simulated or flawed, and this tool could be producing known or predictable keys.
Import your own Private keys generated from a trusted source (QT Wallet)
Addresses and QR codes could be on the printouts could be incorrect.
Manuall verify that this application produces the valid QR codes and addresses
